# GramSeva-Health
"Gram Seva: A Digital Healthcare Bridge for Rural Communities"

Gram Seva is a comprehensive rural healthcare support platform designed to reduce the healthcare gap between villages and urban medical facilities. Many rural communities struggle with delayed medical attention, lack of specialist access, and unorganized pharmacy services. Our solution focuses on making healthcare accessible, structured, and completely digital.
🌿 Key Features of Gram Seva:

• Digital Doctor Appointments:
Users can easily book appointments with available doctors through the website without physically visiting hospitals and waiting in long queues.

• Online Medical Consultations:
Patients can consult doctors virtually, making primary healthcare accessible even in remote areas.

• Digital Pharmacy Management:
Prescriptions are uploaded digitally, medicines can be checked for availability, and pharmacy services are streamlined to avoid confusion and delays.

• Health Record Management:
Patients’ medical history, prescriptions, and reports are securely stored online for easy access and future reference.
